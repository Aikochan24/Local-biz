OUR STORY

Founded on flavor, fueled by community.

Not Your Average Chain

FlashGrill was founded on a simple belief: fast food doesn't have to be processed food. We are a family-owned operation dedicated to serving the Garden Grove community with integrity. Every piece of chicken is hand-breaded, every sauce is made in-house, and every guest is treated like family.

The FlashGrill Story


At FlashGrill, we aren't just in the business of food; we're in the business of people. Founded right here in Garden Grove, our goal has always been to provide a high-quality meal with exceptional service.


Every piece of chicken is hand-breaded in our kitchen and served to you fresh. We take pride in our local roots and our commitment to the community.


Our Values

Quality ingredients, warm hospitality, and a clean environment for every guest.
